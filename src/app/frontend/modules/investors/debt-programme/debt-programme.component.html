Sustainable finance framework

At Redefine, we understand that the decisions we make today impact the legacy we leave tomorrow. To support our value-creation objectives, we are committed to raising financing that is explicitly linked to projects that help manage our climate risks and to advancing our overall ESG strategy and ensuring our long-term resilience. Our sustainability financing framework clearly outlines how we issue and use financial instruments such as green, social and sustainability bonds and loans to achieve our ESG goals.
