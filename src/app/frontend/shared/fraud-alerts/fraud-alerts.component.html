Fraud Alerts

As with any large organisation, Redefine Properties attracts the attention of individuals intent on misusing its name or the names of its employees in the pursuit of fraudulent activities from time to time.

Redefine Properties endeavours to highlight any major frauds that it is aware of on this website as soon as reasonably possible, but unfortunately the increasing volume and type of frauds and scams means that it is impossible to prevent the misuse of Redefine Propertiesâ€™ name and the names of its employees. The most common frauds and scams known to us include, but are not limited to, the below:

  • Email Scams

    • If you receive a communication which purports to be in our name or in the name of our employees and are unsure as to its veracity, please contact us on Enquiries@redefine.co.za before engaging with the sender any further.

  • Banking or Payment Scams

    • If you receive an email or letter or links that appear to be sent from someone you trust at Redefine Properties to convince you to pay money into a fraudulent bank account controlled by them, please take care and before remitting any funds, please contact the person responsible for your account to verify that the bank account you have on record is a legitimate bank account of Redefine Properties.

  • Sale of Shares

    • Redefine Properties will never offer shares for purchase direct to the public, or through the use of social media or instant messaging platforms. If you receive a communication informing you to do so, please do not engage with the sender and report it to Enquiries@redefine.co.za

  • Cryptocurrency or forex trading Scams

    • Redefine Properties does not provide services related to cryptocurrency or any other virtual currency and will never contact any person or entity (e.g., through messages on WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, Facebook, email, text, etc.) about solicitations involving cryptocurrency.

Redefine Properties will not be liable for actions that result in you being a victim of such fraud or scam.

Redefine Properties will report any fraudulent activities involving its name or the names of its employees to the relevant regulatory and law enforcement agencies.
